## Usage Tips

1. **Always include the demo link** - Make it easy for people to try
2. **Use relevant hashtags** - Help people discover your project
3. **Show screenshots** - Visual content performs better
4. **Engage with responses** - Reply to comments and questions
5. **Share metrics** - "45 transactions, Â£927 tracked" is compelling
6. **Highlight the tech** - Developers love knowing the stack
7. **Ask for feedback** - Encourages engagement

## Best Times to Post

- **Twitter/X**: 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-6pm (your timezone)
- **LinkedIn**: Tuesday-Thursday, 7-8am, 12pm, 5-6pm
- **Reddit**: Monday-Friday, 6-8am EST (before US work day)
- **Instagram**: Wednesday, 11am-1pm; Friday, 10-11am
